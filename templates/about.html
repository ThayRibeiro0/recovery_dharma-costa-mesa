About Recovery Dharma Costa Mesa

My name is Maher, and I’m one of the original facilitators of the Recovery Dharma Costa Mesa group. Our journey began during my time in treatment at Barn Life, where I connected with friends also seeking healing and transformation.

One of our friends had started a Refuge Recovery meeting and introduced us to the idea. From there, we discovered Recovery Dharma — a Buddhist-inspired approach to recovery that deeply resonated with us.

I shared the idea with others at Barn Life, and they were excited about hosting a Recovery Dharma meeting on-site. Using the resources and guidance provided by the Recovery Dharma community, we began holding weekly meetings that quickly grew in depth and connection.

We enjoyed a beautiful season of gathering together at Barn Life. Eventually, I found myself holding the space alone for a while, and when I asked for support, the community responded with open hearts — and the group began to grow once more.

Our meetings were later relocated to a new home, and today, we are honored to hold space at the Higashi Honganji Buddhist Temple in Costa Mesa — a peaceful and welcoming environment for our sangha.

The group is now held regularly with the support of compassionate facilitators, including Tommy, Andrea, and Nick, who bring their own wisdom, presence, and kindness to our meetings.

Recovery Dharma Costa Mesa is grounded in meditation, self-inquiry, shared wisdom, and a strong sense of community. Everyone is welcome — wherever you are on your journey, know that you are not alone.
